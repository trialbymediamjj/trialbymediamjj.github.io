TRAILER ARTWORK

On this page, we are delighted to present to you the captivating trailer artwork for the highly anticipated documentary, "Trial by Media: A Michael Jackson Stan Story." These compelling visuals are widely utilized across multiple platforms, including YouTube, Vimeo, and this very site, to generate intrigue and build anticipation for the film.

The artwork for "Trial by Media: A Michael Jackson Stan Story" embraces a distinct horror and sci-fi aesthetic, offering viewers a unique perspective on the portrayal of Michael Jackson stans. While not explicitly monstrous, the artwork portrays these dedicated fans as enigmatic beings straddling the line between obsession and adoration. It seeks to delve into the complexities and intensity of their unwavering loyalty, often venturing into uncharted territory where the boundaries between fandom and fanaticism blur.

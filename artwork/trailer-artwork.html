TRAILER ARTWORK

On this page, you’ll find the gloriously over‑the‑top trailer artwork for the much‑whispered‑about documentary Trial by Media: A Michael Jackson Stan Story. These dramatic visuals are splashed across YouTube, Vimeo, and anywhere else brave enough to host them, all in the noble pursuit of whipping up excitement among people who already spend their evenings arguing with strangers on the internet about the correct pronunciation of “hee‑hee”.

The artwork for Trial by Media: A Michael Jackson Stan Story leans heavily into horror and sci‑fi vibes, offering a fresh angle on the curious species known as the Michael Jackson stan. They’re not depicted as outright monsters—more like mysterious, slightly glowing creatures who’ve spent a bit too long in the comment sections. The imagery captures that delicate balance between devotion and “I’ve printed out 400 screenshots to prove a point nobody asked about”.

It’s an attempt to explore the sheer intensity of their loyalty, which often wanders into territories previously uncharted by science, psychology, or common sense. In this strange landscape, the line between fandom and full‑blown fanaticism becomes so blurry you’d need night‑vision goggles and a support team to find it again

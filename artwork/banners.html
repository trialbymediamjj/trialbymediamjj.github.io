BANNERS

Banners are basically the internet’s version of someone yelling through a megaphone in Tesco car park — impossible to ignore and usually quite entertaining. Whether you’re sharing a solemn proclamation or just trying to give the public a gentle chuckle, banners are the trusty workhorses of online attention‑seeking. And what better excuse to dust them off than to promote the soon‑to‑be‑iconic documentary, Trial by Media: A Michael Jackson Stan Story?

We’ve brewed up a cheeky selection of banners tailor‑made for your social feeds — the sort of thing guaranteed to make even the most dedicated MJ superfan clutch their sequinned glove in dramatic disbelief. Each banner proudly screams: “Glorify the Icon, Demonize the Media.” It’s snappy, it’s satirical, and it lovingly ribs the curious habit some fans have of treating Michael Jackson like a misunderstood archangel while blaming everyone else for… well, everything.

These banners strike that perfect balance between comedy and commentary — the kind of thing that’ll make people laugh, think, and possibly send you a strongly worded DM in full caps. Share them far and wide and you’ll not only grab eyeballs but also plunge head‑first into the bubbling stew of conversations about celebrity worship, media melodrama, and the fans who’d defend MJ with the sort of zeal usually reserved for medieval crusades.

So don’t faff about. Make a splash. Stir the pot. Spark the discourse. Let these banners be your digital foghorn, summoning the masses to explore the gloriously chaotic world of fame, fandom, and the delightful absurdity of Trial by Media: A Michael Jackson Stan Story.

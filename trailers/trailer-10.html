DAWN OF THE STAN

Lucy's ordinary life takes a harrowing turn when a zombie, filled with fanatic zeal for Michael Jackson, attacks her husband. Fleeing for her life, she uncovers a nightmarish reality - her once familiar neighborhood now swarming with the relentless undead. These are not just ordinary zombies; they are zealous devotees of the King of Pop, hell-bent on assimilating humanity into their celebrity worshipping ranks.

As Lucy fights for her survival, she unearths the origins of this "Stan-demic", a virulent force wreaking havoc with catastrophic consequences. Thrust into a treacherous existence where danger lurks at every turn, she must evade the relentless pursuit of the hordes of fanatical stans.

Yet, Lucy is not alone in this struggle. Along her arduous journey, she crosses paths with other resolute survivors, forming an unwavering alliance bonded by the shared goal of defying extinction. Travelling across the nation, they encounter pockets of humanity that still cling to reason amidst the chaos.

Throughout the turmoil, Lucy clings to a steadfast beacon of hope, unwavering in her belief that even in the bleakest hour, there exists a glimmer of optimism. Finally reaching her journey's end, she realizes the profound significance of her odyssey. Not only has she emerged triumphant from the stan apocalypse, but she has also unearthed the resilience and valor residing within her all along.

RETURN OF THE LIVING GLORIFIER

In a tragic turn of events, a serene community was plunged into chaos following the accidental release of a mind-altering gas intended for psychological studies. The gas, originally designed to alleviate fears and uncertainties, instead sparked a nightmarish transformation. Individuals morphed into zealot-like beings, fixated on venerating the legendary pop icon, Michael Jackson.

Once peaceful neighborhoods now teemed with soulless figures, their sole purpose being the adulation of their celebrity idol. The gas permeated the air unchecked, affecting all who breathed it in. Those immune to its effects found themselves pitted against the zealous Michael Jackson stans, forced to fend off relentless attacks or retreat into isolation.

The infected clustered together, relentlessly pursuing those who resisted their zealous convictions. They launched assaults, aiming to coerce non-converts into embracing the Michael Jackson cult. Uninfected individuals faced an ultimatum – engage in a fight for survival or seek refuge by barricading themselves from the world outside.

As time wore on, the situation grew increasingly desperate. The death toll mounted, and essential resources dwindled for the untouched survivors. Amidst the chaos, a glimmer of hope emerged – the quest for a cure. Yet, this pursuit was riddled with challenges, compounded by the conversion of much of the scientific community into zealous followers.

Nevertheless, a resilient group persisted in their pursuit of a remedy. These valiant souls toiled ceaselessly, rigorously testing countless formulas and treatments. Ultimately, their dedication bore fruit as they uncovered an antidote to the stan epidemic – pure, unadulterated common sense. The swift dissemination of this cure brought immense relief and gratitude to the uninfected survivors.

Though the immediate crisis abated, the aftermath left an indelible mark on the world. The trauma and devastation wrought by the cult of celebrity would forever haunt the survivors, a poignant reminder of the cataclysmic events that had transpired.

NIGHT OF THE MORALLY-BANKRUPT

Enter the realm of fandoms, where passionate supporters thrive, and meet a special breed - the Michael Jackson stan. Their fervor for the King of Poop is unparalleled, unyielding, and reminiscent of the relentless zombies from George A. Romero's Classic 1968 film. Welcome to "Night of the Morally-Bankrupt," a twisted tale that delves into a faction driven by delusions and a relentless pursuit of glorifying Michael Jackson at any cost.

In stark contrast to the brain-hungry zombies of popular culture, these individuals are fueled by their own delusions rather than external validation. Their unwavering devotion is driven by an insatiable need to gratify their inflated egos, spinning a web of distorted truth and zealous pursuit.

Prepare to navigate a minefield of delusion when encountering these zealous stans, whether in a dimly lit alleyway or within the vast expanse of the virtual landscape. Engaging in discourse with them mirrors an endeavor fraught with irrationality, where rational arguments are met with a contortion of substantiated evidence to align with their preconceived narratives.

Join us on this captivating and twisted journey as we explore the depths of unwavering devotion, where the boundaries of reality and fiction blur in the relentless pursuit of a distorted truth. In this parody trailer, we present a clash of delusion and reality, offering a glimpse into the minds of those whose fandom knows no bounds. "Night of the Morally-Bankrupt" takes you on a riveting rollercoaster ride through obsession and moral ambiguity, redefining the meaning of Trial by Media.

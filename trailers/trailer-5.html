CAN YOU STAN THE TRUTH?

A mist has descended upon a once serene town, but this is no ordinary mist. It is an ominous presence, thick and abnormal, carrying with it a distinctive smell of glorification rainbow farts. As the mist engulfs the area, a group of shoppers find themselves trapped in a seemingly ordinary store, only to discover that they are not alone.

To their horror, they realize that the mist has brought forth a dark and evil presence, taking the physical form of humanoids. But these are not just any humanoids; they are Michael Jackson glorifiers, a group of fanatics who have surrounded the supermarket, seeking to convert more victims. These humanoids possess an almost supernatural ability, making them dangerous and relentless in their pursuit of turning ordinary people into fervent Michael Jackson stans.

Days turn into weeks as the trapped shoppers struggle to survive amidst dwindling supplies of Pringles and Sunny Delight. It becomes evident that escaping the clutches of the mist and the Michael Jackson glorifiers is an impossible task. The survivors find themselves trapped with no means of escape, while the fanatics seem to gain more power with each passing day.

However, amidst the despair, two individuals, Kenneth and Billy, refuse to yield to hopelessness. They cling to a glimmer of faith, believing that somehow they can find a way to break free from the mist's grip and overcome the relentless Michael Jackson glorifiers.

As they battle for their survival, Kenneth and the group begin to unravel the truth behind this sinister phenomenon. Much to their astonishment, they discover that the Michael Jackson glorifiers are not mindless creatures controlled by the mist, but the physical embodiment of the iconic bed occupier himself.

Armed with this newfound knowledge, Kenneth and the survivors devise a daring plan. They understand that defeating the Michael Jackson glorifiers and liberating themselves from the mist requires confronting the root of the evil and putting an end to it once and for all.

With unwavering determination, Kenneth and the group prepare for the final showdown. They know the journey ahead is fraught with peril, but they refuse to back down. As they face the terrifying humanoids and the mist that holds their town captive, they also hope their courageous struggle will inspire others to stand against the darkness and reclaim their freedom.

Will Kenneth and his comrades succeed in breaking the mist's hold and banishing the Michael Jackson glorifiers? Only time will reveal the outcome of this battle against a nefarious force that threatens to consume their very souls.

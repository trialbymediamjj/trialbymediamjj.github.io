About

Have you ever wondered about the unsettling phenomenon of fandom and the dangerous glorification of celebrities? Trial by Media: A Michael Jackson Stan Story is a thought-provoking documentary that delves deep into this subject, shedding light on the reality of blind devotion and the repercussions of idolizing a celebrity to an almost god-like status.

In today's digital age, social media platforms such as Twitter and Facebook have become breeding grounds for the colonization of Michael Jackson stans. These ardent fans have been known to spread chronic misinformation on a level that even the Russian government would be jealous of. But their influence doesn't stop there. Video sharing platforms like YouTube have become a dark and disturbing path for Michael Jackson stans, where they try to force so-called "factual documentaries" down people's throats. However, these documentaries often turn out to be a massive mismatch of misinformation and demonization.

Trial by Media MJ aims to cast light on these morally bankrupt individuals and provide a different perspective on Michael Jackson and his troubling behavior. It seeks to challenge the constant praise and glorification he receives, which is often accomplished through a combination of demonization and broken tactics.

This documentary serves as a wake-up call, reminding us that celebrities, no matter how big they are, should not be blindly glorified. The film exposes individuals who choose to glorify celebrities, shedding light on their unbelievably sad personas and narcissistic egos.

Join us on this eye-opening journey as we explore the unsettling world of Michael Jackson fandom and the repercussions of idolization. Prepare to question the blind devotion that can cloud judgment and discover the potential dangers that lie within. Trial by Media: A Michael Jackson Stan Story promises to challenge your perspective and spark important conversations about the dark side of idolization.

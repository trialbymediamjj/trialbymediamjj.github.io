On this page, you’re about to feast your eyes on an exclusive buffet of trailers that offer a sneak peek into the wild and wacky universe of Michael Jackson stans. Get ready for the upcoming documentary, "Trial by Media: A Michael Jackson Stan Story," which takes a nosedive into the quirky subculture of fans who have lovingly—and perhaps a little obsessively—crowned the late King of Poop as their idol.

These trailers are a riot, showcasing the bizarre lengths MJ stans will go to in their quest to glorify their iconic weirdo. The film leaves no wild dance move unturned, exploring the fervent, sometimes downright nutty, adoration that runs rampant among these devoted fans. Spoiler alert: you might need a seatbelt for this rollercoaster ride of devotion!

One particularly highlight in many of these trailers is the recurring horror motif. It’s a real treat to see stans portrayed as zombies, not craving brains, but rather the endless glorification of their beloved bed occupier. Move over, zombies of lore; these fans have a different kind of hunger! The bizarre mix of fandom and ghoulish antics adds a delightful twist to the documentary, capturing the otherworldly love fest that is the Michael Jackson stan community.

Get ready to be entertained by the strange dance between the absurd and the adored, as "Trial by Media: A Michael Jackson Stan Story" whisks you away on a thrilling, moonwalk-filled journey into the wonderfully weird world of fandom.

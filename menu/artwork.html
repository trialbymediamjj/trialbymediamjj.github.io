Artwork

Trial by Media: MJ offers not only a compelling narrative, but also incredible artwork that enhances the viewer's experience. The vivid colors and clever commentary showcased in the artwork truly make it a masterpiece. However, what sets this artwork apart is the way it incorporates parody images, specifically aimed at satirizing both the Michael Jackson fans and the tendency to glorify him.

Regardless of whether one is an ordinary viewer or a devout Michael Jackson fan, the artwork displayed in this documentary is undeniably captivating. The level of quality achieved is unparalleled, with each image conveying its own distinct story. Most notably, the attention to detail is truly astonishing. From the facial expressions of the depicted characters to even the minutest elements, everything is captured flawlessly. The emotions portrayed by the characters' expressions are so powerfully conveyed that one can almost feel their presence.

Through the artwork's brilliant wit and clever humor, it offers a fresh and thought-provoking perspective on the world of Michael Jackson. It challenges the notion of blind idolization and prompts reflection on the impact of glorification. The artwork stimulates conversations surrounding the cultural phenomenon surrounding the King of Poop, shedding new light on his bed occupying legacy.
